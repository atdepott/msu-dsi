Tool Description
Using the k Nearest Neighbors clustering method, find clusters in TIF raster files.
Show Sample Data Citation | Hide
HarvestChoice, 2011. "Agricultural production, yield and harvested area (2000)." International Food Policy Research Institute, Washington, DC., and University of Minnesota, St. Paul, MN. Available online at http://harvestchoice.org/maps

Enter the number of clusters you wish to find. The k Nearest Neighbors clustering method may return fewer clusters than were entered. This application supports at most ten clusters.

This tool make take several minutes to run.
